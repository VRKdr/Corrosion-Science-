What is Corrosion?

Corrosion is the gradual disintegration of metals due to chemical or electrochemical reactions with the environment. It causes significant material loss and affects industries worldwide.

Types of Corrosion

  • Dry Corrosion: Occurs in the absence of moisture due to direct reaction with gases like oxygen and sulfur dioxide.
  • Wet Corrosion: Happens in the presence of moisture, forming electrochemical cells leading to metal degradation.

Corrosion Prevention Methods

  • Cathodic Protection: Using sacrificial anodes like zinc to protect the main metal.
  • Protective Coatings: Application of paints, varnishes, and metallic coatings to prevent corrosion.
  • Corrosion Inhibitors: Chemical substances that slow down the corrosion process.
